What's Included

What We Cover & When to Call

Below is a quick summary of what's included under this service, and the most common warning signs that you should book a plumber.

What We Do

Our sewer repair & cleaning service covers:

  • CCTV sewer line inspection and reporting
  • Sewer line jetting and cleaning
  • Tree root removal from sewer mains
  • Trenchless pipe relining (no-dig repair)
  • Section replacement of damaged sewer pipes
  • Pre-purchase sewer inspections for property buyers

Signs You Should Call

Don't wait until the problem gets worse. Call us if you notice:

  • Multiple drains blocked at once
  • Sewage smell inside or outside the home
  • Gurgling toilets when other fixtures are used
  • Slow draining throughout the whole house
  • Wet, sunken or unusually green patches in the lawn
  • Sewage backing up into showers or floor wastes
How It Works

Our Simple 4-Step Process

No surprises, no hidden costs. Here's exactly how we handle your job from the first call to the final walkthrough.

1

Camera Inspection

We push a high-resolution camera through the line to see exactly what's going on underground.

2

Diagnosis & Quote

You get a written report, a video of the issue, and a clear repair quote.

3

Repair the Right Way

Wherever possible we use trenchless relining — no excavation, no torn-up garden.

4

Verify & Sign Off

Final camera inspection confirms the line is fully repaired and flowing free.

Why Choose Rivus

Why Customers Trust Us With This Job

Tree roots and old earthenware sewer lines are common across older Northern Beaches homes. Our trenchless relining can save you thousands compared to digging up the lawn or driveway — and gives you a 50-year pipe inside the old one.
