The Problem

When Hot Water Fails, You Need Help Fast

A failed hot water system is more than an inconvenience — it can disrupt your entire household. Whether your system has stopped working entirely, is delivering lukewarm water, or is leaking from the tank, Rivus Plumbing can diagnose and resolve the issue fast.

We work with all brands and all system types. If repair isn't cost-effective, we'll provide honest advice on the best replacement option for your home size, budget, and energy preferences — including energy-efficient heat pump and solar options.

System Types

Hot Water Systems We Service

⚡

Electric Hot Water

The most common system in Australian homes. We repair thermostats, elements, and valves, or replace the unit with an energy-efficient upgrade.

🔥

Gas Hot Water

Instant and continuous-flow gas systems provide hot water on demand. We service and install all major gas system brands.

☀️

Solar Hot Water

Solar systems reduce running costs significantly. We service roof collectors, storage tanks, and booster elements to keep your system efficient.

🌡️

Heat Pump Systems

Heat pumps are the most energy-efficient option available. We supply and install leading brands with full government rebate assistance.

🔧

Repairs & Servicing

Leaking tanks, faulty tempering valves, anode rod replacement, pressure relief valve checks — we service all components.

🏗️

Full Replacements

When repair isn't cost-effective, we remove and install a new system, handling all plumbing connections and commissioning.
